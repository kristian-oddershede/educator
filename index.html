About Me

With more than 4,000 hours of experience in teaching and science communication, I’ve worked across schools, universities, and science centers — helping people of all ages connect with scientific thinking.

I hold an MSc in Physics from Aarhus University and have collaborated with CERN on developing training programs for their science guides.

  • At Være Ung: Volunteer organization leader working to support youth well-being and inclusion.
  • Science Museums, Aarhus: Science communication that makes complex ideas accessible and exciting.
  • CERN Collaboration: Co-developer of interactive training for science communicators and educators.
