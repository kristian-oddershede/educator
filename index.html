Kristian Dahlmann Oddershede

Educator and facilitator

I have more than 4000 hours of experience in practical Science Communication and Education. Working with schools, universities and science museums to create interactive learning spaces and foster hands-on education. Most recently I helped CERN (European Organisation for Nuclear Research) in restructuring how they train their science guides for the task of engageing with visitors. My formal background is a Master's degree in physics from Aarhus University in Denmark.

Volunteer Organisation

I’m passionate about youth engagement and community building. I manage At Være Ung — a volunteer organisation that creates meaningful opportunities for young people to connect and share their stories to help combate mental health issues in youth.
